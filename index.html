How to Use Comment Sanitizer

            
            

                The Comment Sanitizer app is designed to be simple and effective. Here's a quick guide on how to use it:
            

            

                1. Sanitize a Comment: Go to the Home tab. Type or paste your comment into the text area. Click the Sanitize Comment button. The app will automatically replace any inappropriate words with asterisks (*). A message will appear showing you which words were sanitized.
            

            

                2. Sanitize a Text File: If you have a `.txt` file with comments, go to the Text File Sanitizer tab. Click Choose File, select your file, and then click Sanitize File. The sanitized content will be displayed on the screen. You can then download the clean version of the file using the Download Sanitized File button.
            

            

                3. View History: The History tab keeps a record of all the comments you've sanitized during your session.

About Comment Sanitizer

            
            

                Comment Sanitizer is a powerful and easy-to-use tool designed to help individuals and
                businesses keep online conversations clean and respectful. It detects and masks inappropriate words,
                ensuring your content remains family-friendly and professional.
            

            

                This app helps by scanning text inputs or uploaded files and replacing bad words with asterisks (*),
                so your platforms stay welcoming for all users.
            

            

                Integration: Comment Sanitizer can be integrated into social media platforms,
                blogs, forums, or any comment-based system to automatically sanitize user-generated content,
                promoting positive online interactions.
